Work process

A clear sequence before the first working day

Trust in renovation comes from predictability. We start with a site visit, clarify the work and leave a clear trail of what was agreed.

  1. Request

    You send a short description, location and photos if available.

  2. Site visit

    We check the condition of the space and the important technical details.

  3. Offer

    You receive a breakdown by work, materials, timing and terms.

  4. Plan

    We arrange the stages, access, deliveries and critical deadlines.

  5. Execution

    We work in stages and communicate when changes or decisions are needed.

  6. Control

    We follow quality, details, cleanliness and alignment with the offer.

  7. Handover

    We make a final review and clarify support or next steps.
