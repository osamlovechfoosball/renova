Privacy Policy

This policy explains how Pechkov Renova processes personal data when you request renovation, finishing works, a site visit, an offer or follow-up communication.

Controller and contact

The data controller is the trader operating the website and services under the Pechkov Renova brand. For personal data questions you can use phone 0885 000 544.

What data we process

When you make an inquiry, we may process name, phone number, city or neighborhood, site type, description of the work, photos or files of the site, and communication content by phone, Viber, WhatsApp or form.

Why we use the data

  • to return a call or message;
  • to arrange a site visit;
  • to assess the work from photos or description;
  • to prepare an offer and work plan;
  • to perform agreed work, issue invoices and meet accounting obligations when there is an order.

Legal basis

Data is processed mainly to take steps before a contract and to perform a contract. For accounting or legal obligations the basis is a legal requirement. In limited cases legitimate interest may apply, for example to prove communication, protect rights in a dispute or maintain system security.

Who data may be shared with

Data is not sold and is not used for mass marketing. It may be accessible to a hosting or form provider, accountant, legal adviser or subcontractor only when needed for a site visit, offer, execution or legal obligation. If you choose Viber or WhatsApp, communication passes through the respective platform and its rules.

Retention period

Inquiries that do not lead to an order are kept only for a reasonable period for follow-up and reference, usually up to 12 months. Offers, contracts, invoices and documents for completed work are kept according to applicable accounting, tax and limitation periods.

Your rights

You have rights of access, correction, deletion, restriction, objection and portability where applicable under GDPR. You can submit a request through the listed phone number. You also have the right to complain to the Bulgarian Commission for Personal Data Protection.

Photos and files

Send only photos and documents needed for a visit or offer. Do not send identity documents, bank cards, medical data or other sensitive data.
