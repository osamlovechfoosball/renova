Cookie Policy

Cookies are small text files that a website may store in a visitor's browser. They can be used for technical site operation, remembering preferences or statistics.

What cookies this website uses

In its current version, the Pechkov Renova website does not use advertising, analytics or tracking cookies. Google Analytics, Meta Pixel or another marketing system has not been added.

Is a cookie banner needed?

Because the website does not place non-essential cookies, a consent banner has not been added. If analytics, advertising pixels, chat tools or other external services are added in the future, this policy must be updated and a prior consent mechanism must be added where required.

Technical data

The hosting provider may process technical logs such as IP address, date, time, browser and requested files for security and normal site operation. This data is not used by Pechkov Renova for behavioral advertising.
